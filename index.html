Red's Confections started as most ventures do, chasing a passion for baking and it shows in the quality and flavors of everything Red bakes. Red learned about baking from her grandmother at a young age and has always loved being in a kitchen creating things that make people happy.

When she is not in the kitchen baking or filling orders, she has an awesome husband named Seth she spends her time with, notably around Halloween while they're figuring out the next costume idea they want to wear together.
